First nike shoes ever

Crafted by co-founder Bill Bowerman in 1971 using a waffle iron to create a unique sole pattern for better traction, the first Nike shoes, known as the Nike "Moon Shoes," were pivotal in revolutionizing athletic footwear. These pioneering shoes marked the beginning of Nike's journey, propelling the brand towards iconic status within the sports industry. Their innovative design set a new standard for performance and style, cementing Nike's position as a leader in athletic apparel and footwear.
